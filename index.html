NORTHERN SUITES HOSPITALITY

Effectiveness and efficiency, backed by a truly international team of industry professionals , world-class standards and the warmth of Indian hospitality perfected over centuries are the corner stones of our offering. With a cumulative industry (Hospitality & Real Estate) experience of over 30 years among the promoters, NORTHERN SUITES Hospitality is one of the new entrant leading hospitality management companies, with a portfolio that includes over 100 operational suites and rooms and more new properties rapidly being brought onboard.


NORTHERN SUITES combines the expertise of a truly global management team with the hospitality excellence for which India is renowned for. We pride ourselves on an open, approachable leadership style and recognize that our customers and property partners are the fundamental basis for the success of any hotel management company. Their needs and concerns are our driving force.
